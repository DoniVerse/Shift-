Ethical Code of Conduct – Students

By joining SHIFT's platform and participating in any job placement, the student agrees to uphold the following ethical principles:

1. Integrity

  • Always act honestly and avoid any form of deception, cheating, or misinformation.
  • Represent one's qualifications, skills, and performance truthfully.

2. Accountability

  • Take full responsibility for the quality and timeliness of your work.
  • Own up to mistakes, seek guidance when unsure, and correct errors proactively.

3. Confidentiality

  • Maintain the privacy of company information, client data, and any non-public details encountered during assignments.
  • Do not share or reproduce sensitive materials without written consent.

4. Fairness and Non-Discrimination

  • Treat all individuals and team members with equal respect, regardless of gender, race, religion, or background.
  • Do not engage in gossip, favoritism, or toxic behavior in the workplace.

5. Professional Independence

  • Avoid conflicts of interest. Do not use your role or access for personal, political, or unethical gain.

6. Respect for Institutions

  • Honor the rules of both the host company and your academic institution.
  • Do not engage in work that compromises your student obligations or violates university policies.

Violation of Ethical Standards may lead to:

  • Written warnings or removal from SHIFT placements
  • Blacklisting from future jobs via SHIFT
  • Referral to your academic department or legal authorities in serious cases
