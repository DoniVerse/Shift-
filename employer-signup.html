ETHICAL CODE OF CONDUCT – COMPANIES

By partnering with SHIFT and hosting student workers, the company agrees to abide by the following ethical principles:

1. Transparency

  • Clearly communicate job expectations, compensation, timelines, and working conditions before placement begins.
  • Avoid misleading job titles or changing terms mid-assignment without proper agreement.

2. Fair Compensation

  • Pay student workers fairly for the work delivered.
  • Uphold commission terms with SHIFT (10–15%) and do not circumvent agreements or deny rightful payments.

3. Respect and Non-Exploitation

  • Do not use student labor for unpaid or excessive tasks beyond the agreed scope.
  • Refrain from requesting personal favors, coercive behavior, or any kind of harassment.

4. Confidentiality

  • Protect students' private data and academic identities.
  • Never share or misuse personal or institutional information gained through SHIFT.

5. Commitment to Mentorship

  • Support the student's growth by providing a safe space for learning and professional development.
  • Offer timely feedback and evaluations to promote improvement.

Ethical violations by companies may result in:

  • Immediate termination of the agreement
  • Public or private blacklisting from SHIFT's partnership network
  • Legal and institutional follow-up in cases of serious breach
